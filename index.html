About Convocation

The convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Center

continues the tradition within academic institutions of gathering together academicians, students, alumni, benefactors, and industry professionals as stakeholders of university community. It is the time to exchange views on topical issues that touch teaching, research, and professional development. Every year we select a topic as the standing point to reflect the theme of the present year's 18th of November African Statistics Day. Some of the theme says "Modernizing data ecosystem to accelerate the implementation of African Continental Free Trade Area(AfCFTA): The role of official Statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development of Africa." African Statistics Day is celebrated each year on 18th of November. The commemoration aims among others, to increase public awareness about the important role which Statistics play in all aspects of social and economic development.
